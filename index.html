ABOUT

We're Scarpa! One of Northlands favourite party bands. We play a very cool mix of songs that will get your crowd into party mode.

OUR AIM

Our aim is to create an amazing atmosphere, where the whole crowds involved. Not suitable for background music or quiet events.

WHERE TO FIND US

We play for everyone, with our past performances including memorable appearances at the Friter Festival and The Sandbank Party.
You can hire us for weddings and private events.
